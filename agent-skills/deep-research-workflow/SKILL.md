# Deep Research Workflow

## Use When

- Evaluating new data sources or channels.
- Comparing legal/ToS constraints across platforms.
- Producing evidence-backed strategy notes.

## Workflow

1. Define research question and decision target.
2. Collect evidence from primary/official sources first.
3. Classify findings into: technical feasibility, legal risk, operational cost.
4. Recommend integration path: crawl, API, partner feed, licensed data, manual.
5. Convert findings into `TASKS.md` slices and risk notes.

## Output Template

```text
Research question:
Sources reviewed:
Key evidence:
Feasibility assessment:
Compliance assessment:
Recommended integration path:
Task slices to add:
```

## Guardrails

- Flag uncertain legal interpretations explicitly.
- Avoid conclusions without source-backed evidence.
