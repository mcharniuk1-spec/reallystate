# Investor Pitch (YC Standard)

## Use When

- Creating or rewriting investor decks.
- Structuring startup narrative for fundraising.
- Reworking slides to avoid clutter and overlap.

## Required Story Flow

1. Problem (pain is urgent and expensive)
2. Solution (product and wedge)
3. Why now (market timing)
4. Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM with assumptions)
5. Product demo/value proposition
6. Business model and unit economics
7. Go-to-market
8. Traction/progress
9. Competition and moat
10. Team
11. Ask and milestones

## Slide Rules

- One key message per slide.
- Include at least one paragraph per slide (3-6 sentences).
- Use evidence lines for factual claims (source names and date window).
- Keep text and chart zones separated; no visual overlap.
- Prefer 16:9 layouts for PPTX and landscape for PDF.
