Apartment with sea view in Sveti Vlas

Sveti Vlas

One bedroom apartment on the first line

One-bedroom apartment in in Sveti Vlas, just 50 meters above the beach, at a convenient distance from the main street of the resort with supermarkets, restaurants, shops and all services. This is a small complex of two buildings on 4 floors, with white welcoming facade and arches on the balconies. The two buildings face the sea and between them is a green courtyard with a swimming pool.

The property is located on the 3rd floor with an area of 58.40 sq.m.:
- entrance hall
- living room with kitchenette
- bedroom
- bathroom with toilet
- two balconies

The apartment is bright and cozy with a nice view of the sea and the pool of the complex. It is fully furnished.

Sveti Vlas attracts thousands of tourists every year with its mild climate, sunny beaches and many entertainment opportunities. Sveti Vlas is a favorite place for all those looking for quality recreation and excellent amenities. Many guests decide to make it their home to enjoy the tranquility and sea atmosphere all year round. The beach combines crystal clear sea water with fine sand along the coast.

View of the property
We can arrange a viewing of the property at your convenience. For this purpose, contact the broker responsible for the offer and tell him when you would like to make an inspection.

Reservation of the property
The property can be reserved and taken off the market with payment of a deposit, after which viewings with other buyers will cease and the preparation of the documents for a preliminary and final contract will begin. Please contact the responsible broker for this property for details of the purchase procedure and payment arrangements.
